Hi, My name is Steven Fenczik and I am the creator of the Guitarbacus!
The Idea for the Guitarbacus came to me in college after thousands of hours of jamming and music research. I've always believed that anyone can play amazing improvised music if they set their minds to it, but I acknowledge that the information out there can be disjointed and confusing. Music education is notoriously arcane.

The Guitarbacus is here to help dispell the mystery as best I can.

To see the Guitarbacus in action. Visit my youtube channel at youtube.com/fenczikstudios. I am regularly posting new improvised music, music discussion podcasts and more!
